View and manage all orders across your trading accounts. Track order status, cancel pending orders, and review order history.

  • Open and filled order history

  • Order type, action, and status display

  • Cancel pending orders with confirmation

  • Display toggle: lots or units

  • Only active accounts are shown (toggle on the Accounts page)

  • Filters

    • User (admin only) - show orders for specific users

    • Broker - show orders from specific brokers only

    • Start/End Date - narrow down to a date range

    • Symbol - show orders for specific currency pairs

    • Action - show only Buy or Sell orders

    • Type - show only Market, Limit, or Stop orders

    • Status - show Filled, Pending, or Cancelled orders
